Study Notes

Cell Structure & Function

Cell Theory: All living organisms are composed of cells, the basic unit of life.

Organelles: Nucleus (DNA storage), Mitochondria (ATP production), Ribosomes (Protein synthesis), ER (Transport), Golgi Apparatus (Packaging), Lysosomes (Digestion).

Transport: Diffusion, Osmosis, Active Transport, Endocytosis, Exocytosis.

DNA & Protein Synthesis

Mutations: Substitutions, Insertions, Deletions, Frameshifts.

Macromolecules & Water Properties

Carbohydrates: Energy source (glucose, starch, cellulose).

Lipids: Energy storage, cell membranes (phospholipids).

Proteins: Enzymes, structure, transport.

Nucleic Acids: DNA & RNA (genetic information).

Water Properties: Polarity, Cohesion, Adhesion, Universal Solvent.

Meiosis & Heredity

Meiosis: Produces haploid gametes (sperm, egg) for sexual reproduction.

Punnett Squares: Predicts inheritance of traits.

Dominant vs. Recessive: Traits controlled by alleles.

The Cell Cycle & Cancer

Cell Cycle: G1 (growth), S (DNA replication), G2 (prep), Mitosis (division).

Mitosis: Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase, Cytokinesis.

Cancer: Uncontrolled cell division due to mutations.
